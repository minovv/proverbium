Why do you need to learn proverbs and idioms?

Understanding proverbs and idioms can boost your conversational skills. You may encounter proverbs and idioms most often in spoken or written conversation. Proverbs and idioms can help improve your conversational skills because it shows native speakers that you understand the cultural meaning and context behind them.
