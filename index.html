Proverbium

Why do you need to learn proverbs and idioms?

Do you learning German like me? Here is the Pro tip: "Try to learn proverbs and idioms as much as you can."

Understanding proverbs and idioms can boost your conversational skills. You may encounter proverbs and idioms most often in spoken or written conversation. They can help improve your conversational skills because it shows native speakers that you understand the cultural meaning and context behind them.

The Lego-like grammatical structure of the German language makes it possible to combine an inordinate number of elements, so it is not uncommon to describe an extremely specific concept with a single, brutally long word in German (for example, Donaudampfschiffahrtsgesellschaftskapitän — 42 letters).
